Privacy Protocol

Your privacy and data security are of utmost importance to the DOSA CTF Administration. Please review our privacy practices below:

1. Information Collection

  • We collect your Callsign (Username) and Email address during registration.
  • We log IP addresses, login timestamps, and platform actions for security and auditing purposes.

2. Data Usage

  • Your data is solely used to manage your account, calculate your ranking on the leaderboard, and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Security Measures

  • Passwords are securely hashed using modern cryptographic algorithms.
  • Our platform employs encryption and strict access controls to protect your data.

4. Account Deletion

  • You may request account deletion by contacting the CTF Administrators. Note that deleted accounts will have their scores preserved anonymously to maintain leaderboard integrity.

By participating in DOSA CTF, you consent to this Privacy Protocol.
