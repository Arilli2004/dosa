Rules of Engagement

Welcome to DOSA CTF. To ensure a fair and competitive environment for all operatives, please adhere to the following rules:

1. Authorized Targets Only

  • Only attack the infrastructure and IP addresses specifically provided within the challenge descriptions.
  • Do not attack the CTF platform itself, other teams, or external infrastructure.

2. No Flag Sharing

  • Sharing flags, writeups, or solutions before the CTF concludes is strictly prohibited.
  • Collaboration is only allowed within your registered team (if applicable).

3. Denial of Service (DoS)

  • Do not perform any Denial of Service (DoS) or Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks against any infrastructure.
  • Automated scanning tools (e.g., DirBuster, SQLMap) are allowed only on challenge infrastructure, but please throttle your requests to avoid disruption.

4. Reporting Issues

  • If you discover a bug or vulnerability in the CTF platform itself, please report it to the admins immediately. Do not exploit it.

5. Conduct

  • Maintain professional and respectful conduct in all communications. Harassment or abuse will result in an immediate ban.

Failure to comply with these rules will result in immediate disqualification and account ban.
